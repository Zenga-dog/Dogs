In practices I would always get very tired and felt like walking all the time. Yet with my determination, I was able to get 8th place in my first meet. But I still wasn't showing what I could really do. In the last meet of the season I got 3rd place as well as in the leagu finals. I carried on and trained harder than before to prepare for a challenging competition. The county finals. The course had steeps hills that went up and down cuntiously. It was the most tiring course I ever ran. But I never gave up. I continued till I reached the finish line and got 10th place overall. In the Mid April of 2016, I had joined track and field. There I competed in the 1600 meters run(the mile) and the 4 by 400 meters relay. Since I wanted to try other events out as well, I joined long jump and triple jump. After weeks of training and improving times for mile, 4 by 400 meters relay, and triple jump, I was accepted into league finals. In triple jump I didn't do so good, but for mile I got 7th place. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to make it to county finals. But there was still one event left. The relay.
